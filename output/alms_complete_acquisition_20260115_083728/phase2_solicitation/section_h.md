## H.2 CYBERSECURITY REQUIREMENTS

### H.2.1 NIST 800-171 Compliance
The contractor shall implement security controls per NIST 800-171 Rev 2 - "Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in Nonfederal Systems and Organizations."

**Required Controls:** All 110 security requirements in NIST 800-171

### H.2.2 CMMC Certification
**CMMC Level Required:** Level 2

The contractor shall achieve and maintain CMMC Level 2 certification within 12 months of contract award.

### H.2.3 Cybersecurity Clauses
The following DFARS cybersecurity clauses apply:
- DFARS 252.204-7008 - Compliance with Safeguarding Covered Defense Information Controls
- DFARS 252.204-7012 - Safeguarding Covered Defense Information and Cyber Incident Reporting
- DFARS 252.204-7019 - Notice of NIST SP 800-171 DoD Assessment Requirements
- DFARS 252.204-7020 - NIST SP 800-171 DoD Assessment Requirements

### H.2.4 Incident Reporting
Cyber incidents shall be reported to DoD within 72 hours per DFARS 252.204-7012.
